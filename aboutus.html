ABOUT BRAND

Moving around the world, inspired by the cultures of different countries, studying and mastering the most valuable techniques of leather craft, in 2018 we created the DIAMOND brand, embodying authenticity, passion and freedom of expression.
Creating a new bag model, thinking about color and texture, we present the image of a modern woman - strong and independent, free from other people's assessments, creative, wild in her nature, who, despite prejudices, changes the world and brings her invaluable contribution to society. We, in turn, want to show that any woman, wherever she is, has the legal right to be herself!

PRODUCTION

In an era of rapid technological development and global mechanization, we favor centuries-old production techniques.

We strive to maintain and develop handicraft skills, build relationships with small family factories, contributing to the preservation of the craft. Each of our bags is designed and manufactured in Italy by experienced craftsmen who have been creating leather goods that are like a work of art for generations.

Quality is not accidental, it is the result of carefully thought-out processes and the work of professionals. We spend a lot of time and effort developing and improving the design and achieving a high level.

REASONABLE CONSUMPTION

We work only with reliable and responsible suppliers from Italy. All reptile skin comes with the necessary CITES documents. We strive for responsible production and call for conscious consumption.
